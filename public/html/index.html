RANGEELO RAAS

ABOUT

Rangeelo Raas, founded in the spring of 2017 by a group of raas-garba enthusiasts at the University of Florida, is Northern Florida's first official national level Raas-Garba dance competition. While hoping to show fellow raasers from around the country some true southern hospitality, Rangeelo Raas aims to promote the presence of cultural values in Gujarati folk dance throughout universities nationwide and raise money towards its philanthropy.
